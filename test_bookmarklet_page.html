Advanced Laboratory Microscope XR-2000

High-precision optical microscope with digital imaging capabilities for research and education.

The XR-2000 is a state-of-the-art laboratory microscope designed for both professional research and educational applications. Featuring advanced optics, digital imaging integration, and precise mechanical controls, this instrument delivers exceptional performance for various microscopy applications.

XR-2000-Basic XR-2000-Pro XR-2000-Digital

Key Features

  • High-resolution optical system with advanced lens technology
  • Integrated digital camera with 4K video recording
  • Precision mechanical stage with micrometer controls
  • LED illumination system with adjustable intensity
  • Multiple magnification options from 40x to 1000x
  • Compatible with various sample preparation techniques
Optical Specifications

Magnification: 40x, 100x, 400x, 1000x

Objective Lenses: 4x, 10x, 40x, 100x (oil immersion)

Eyepieces: 10x wide field

Illumination: LED with adjustable brightness

Physical Specifications

Dimensions: 350mm (W) x 250mm (D) x 450mm (H)

Weight: 8.5 kg

Stage Size: 140mm x 110mm

Power: 12V DC adapter included

Accessories

  • Dust cover
  • Calibration slide
  • Immersion oil
  • USB cable for digital connectivity
  • Software CD with imaging software

Application Areas

  • Biology and life sciences research
  • Materials science analysis
  • Quality control in manufacturing
  • Educational institutions
  • Medical diagnostics
